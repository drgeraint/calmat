This is the CALMAT Player

The CALMAT Player can deliver tutorial material written for you by your tutor in the form of Learning Plans. These are HTML pages which include links to MoLS Sections and TAS Questions.

To see a list of the Learning Plans made available to you by your tutor, click on the "Learning Plans" button to the left of this window.

If you have taken a CALMAT diagnostic test, you can open your personal Revision Plan by clicking the "My Revision Plan" button.
